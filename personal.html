INFORMATION ABOUT ADAM SSEBATTA

Adam Ssebatta is a son of Mr. Kimera Joseph and Mrs. Nansamba Mary of Kiweesa-Ssingo Mityana. Adam is a practising Muslim. He was born in Mityana to Ugandan parents who migrated to Busunju from Mityana in 1980. He grew up in a small apartment in uganda. His father died shortly after Adam turned 11.

Adam Ssebatta was born as the first child of four brothers and sisters. His dad died when he was very little. From a very young age, the sense of responsibility befell him. The death of his dad left Ssebatta Adam's mother with the saddling burden of parenting.
